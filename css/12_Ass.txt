Pseudo-classes select elements based on a specific state or condition that the elements are in. They are denoted by a colon (":") followed by the pseudo-class name. 

Examples of pseudo-classes:

    :hover: Selects an element when the user hovers over it.
    :active: Selects an element when it is being clicked or activated.
    :first-child: Selects the first child element of its parent.


Pseudo-elements target specific parts of an element's content or structure.Denoted by ::

Ex - 

    ::before: Inserts content before the element's content.
    ::after: Inserts content after the element's content.
